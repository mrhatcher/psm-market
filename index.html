About Us

A neighborhood staple, run by people who care

PSM Market isn't a faceless chain — it's a family-run neighborhood stop on Hwy 54 West in Chapel Hill. Jay and the team have built a reputation for being the friendliest gas station on this stretch of road, and it shows in every interaction.

Whether you're a commuter grabbing coffee, a student stopping for a quick bite, or a neighbor picking up lottery tickets on your way home — you're going to get a smile and honest service every single time.
